Outgoing Flights

As one would expect outgoing and incoming flights are very related; states have similar numbers of people flying in and out. While popular vacation states such as California and Florida show a lot of traffic, states such as Texas are also very high. This suggests that our data might also be affected by state population size.

Flight Fares

Lastly, we wanted to examine average flight fares per state and major cities.

Market Fare of States

Interestingly, we found that the market fare doesn't correlate at all with flight traffic. Hawaii has the highest fare suggesting we might see a correlation if we looked at fare vs. distance traveled. For the most part, however, it seems average fares are very similar for most states.
